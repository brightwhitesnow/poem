Winter





By: Tuan (Ben) Nguyen <-- This is a link!




Winter. The Season of bright white snow.
In the season of winter, sometimes, Snowstorm!
Winter. Winter. Winter.
Shining and glittering with the snow.
Russhing through the air with wind.
The wind howls and howls and howls.
Until... It becomes calm.
Next day, the snow is sad.
The sunny weather, has destroyed the sight, of beauty.
But another winter and snowy day will come.
